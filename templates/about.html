About

Bearing is a free application and our goal is to help you to learn a new language and maximize your learning process with flash cards that can be reviewed anytime you want. Flash cards can also be used for learning or recall anything you want, bearing it in your mind. It is available on its web version only yet, however, we hope we can maximize its compatibility and increase user's experience as we can. Suggestions are welcome and you can contact us by our e-mail at the bottom of the page.

This project was presented as my CS50x's final project, a Harvard Computer Science course teached by David J. Malan and Brian Yu.
